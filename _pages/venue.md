## Venue & Accommodations

### Venue
[Victor Menezes Convention Centre (VMCC)](https://www.iitb.ac.in/article/victor-menezes-convention-centre-vmcc) 

Located at the prestigious Indian Institute of Technology Bombay, the Victor Menezes Convention Centre (VMCC) serves as the venue for IPC 2025. The institute is situated in Powai, Mumbai, offering modern facilities and convenient connectivity to major transportation hubs in the city.

### Campus Facilities

Facilities on campus include a post office, two banks, a shopping center, playgrounds and swimming pools, eating places, two excellent schools and a well-equipped hospital.

### About Mumbai

IIT Bombay is located at Powai, which is an eastern suburb in the North-Eastern part of Mumbai.

Mumbai is in the form of a long narrow island, almost a peninsula, thrusting south wards into the Arabian Sea. It can be broadly divided into four zones.

South Bombay (Colaba, CST,Fort, Chruchgate, Nariman Point etc.)
Central Bombay (Dadar, Bombay Central, Worli etc)
The Western suburbs (Bandra, Juhu, Andheri, Borivali etc)
The Eastern Suburbs(Kurla, Chembur, Ghatkopar, Mulund, Kanjur Marg, Vikhroli etc.)

There is also New Bombay (Vashi, Turbhe etc.) across Thane Creek on the mainland.

**Transportation**: IITB is well connected by cabs to almost all hotel locations.
Note: Most good hotels in the vicinity of IIT campus have tariff upwards of ₹4,500 (US $65) per day. Delegates are advised to book directly with hotels or through preferred booking platforms.

### On-Campus Guest Houses

## Travel to Venue

### From Airport

The closest international airport is Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj International Airport: BOM, roughly 5.6 miles (9.1 kilometers) from conference venue. The conference venue is about a 30 - 40 minutes cab ride from BOM, and the city center is about 1 hour 30 minutes away from the BOM.

### From Railway Station

The closest railway station is Kanjurmarg Station (Central Railway Line), roughly 1.25 miles (2 kilometers) from conference venue.
